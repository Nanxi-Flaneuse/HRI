Welcome to the Robotics Study

Thank you for participating! Please read the following instructions carefully before begining.

  • Complete the Pre-Study Survey before starting the study.
  • During each study session, use the Study Interaction page to record your data. Press the "Start Study" button when begining a study session and "Stop Study" when finished with your study session. Each time you get up to stretch, press the "Stretched" button.
  • Complete the Post-Study Survey after completing all 5 days of the study.
