#Instructions Robotics Study Instructions
Instructions Pre-Study Survey Study Interaction Mid-Study Survey Post-Study Survey

Welcome to the Sphero Robotics Study!

Thank you for participating! Please read the following instructions carefully before begining:

  1. Complete the Pre-Study Survey before starting the study.

  2. During each study session, use the Study Interaction page to record your data.
    • Click the "Start Study" button when begining your study session.
    • Click "Stop Study" when finished with your study session.
    • Each time you get up to stretch, click the "Stretched" button.

  3. Complete the Mid-Study Survey after you have completed five days with your Sphero.

  4. Complete the Post-Study Survey after completing all ten days with your Sphero.
